Earth Observatory Data Cube for Nizamabad district

Using the satellite images of Nizamabad district that are procured from Sentinel 2 and Landsat sensors, we've performed time series analysis, produced analysis-ready data (ARD), and organised the data as multidimensional datacubes. The ARD results from processing satellite imagery from data acquisition to radiometric calibration and, through additional conversions, to top-of-atmosphere (TOA) reflectance and finally surface reflectance. The data cube provides data through web services that could be used by different applications; here, we've used it for the identification and assessment of deforestation in an area of interest such as Nizamabad.

Technologies: Python, Deep Learning, MERN
